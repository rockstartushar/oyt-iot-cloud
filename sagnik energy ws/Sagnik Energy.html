What We Offer

On-Demand Fueling 24x7

We provide on-demand fuel delivery service 24x7 to cars, trucks, generators, fleet and construction. Customers can download our Android app, place order and get fuel delivered at their doorstep! They simply need to enter the quantity and time and pay from avriety of options.

Customers can monitor their fuel consumptions, analyse their fuel usage, generate reports for audit directly from the App. We will soon begin our operations in various places in West Bengal. We serve schools, small machine vans, earth movers, construction sites, bus and truck terminals. On-demand fueling has many benefits: save trips to the pumps, eliminate cash handling and fuel theft and monitor your energy use for being more energy efficient.

Please get in touch with us for your fueling requirements.

Mera Pump

We are creating an ecosystem to lower the entry barrier to pump business. We provide an exciting opportunity to generate income with our franchising platform where we provide the fueling browser, our Android application and the necessary traning.

There are various places where energy is not readily accessible. Enterprising individuals can easily tap into this market by opening their own pumps. The fueling browsers also provide related services (such as checking air pressure etc.) to make the service more compelling. Franchisees will have significant freedom in customizing the business to suit their local needs.

If you would like to know more about our franchising options, please get in touch with us.
